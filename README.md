# 🛡️ PhishGuard — Phishing Awareness Training Module

An interactive, web-based cybersecurity training platform designed to educate users about phishing attacks, social engineering tactics, and online safety. Built with a premium dark-mode interface featuring glassmorphism aesthetics, smooth animations, and a fully responsive layout.

### 🔗 [Live Demo](https://faraz-aamir.github.io/Phishing_Awareness_Module/)

---

## 📌 About

PhishGuard is a single-page educational module that walks users through the world of phishing — from understanding what it is, to recognizing real-world attack patterns, to testing their knowledge through an interactive quiz. The goal is to make cybersecurity awareness engaging, visually compelling, and easy to understand for everyone.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Modern Dark UI** — Premium flat dark-mode design with muted sage-green accents, glassmorphism cards, and Hanken Grotesk typography
- **Animated Statistics** — Live-counting hero stats (3.4B phishing emails/day, 36% of breaches, $4.9M avg. cost)
- **Educational Content** — Clear breakdown of what phishing is, how it works, who's targeted, and the impact
- **6 Attack Types Covered** — Email Phishing, Spear Phishing, Whaling, Smishing (SMS), Vishing (Voice), and Clone Phishing — each with visual risk-level indicators
- **Real-World Examples** — Interactive simulated phishing email (PayPa1 scam) and fake login page (Arnazon portal) with annotated red flags
- **8 Protection Tips** — Actionable best practices including URL verification, 2FA, email filters, and incident reporting
- **10-Question Interactive Quiz** — Covers phishing identification, social engineering, typosquatting, BEC attacks, and incident response — with instant feedback and detailed explanations
- **Fully Responsive** — Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Scroll Animations** — Intersection Observer-based reveal animations and animated risk bars

## 🧠 Quiz Topics

| # | Topic |

| 2 | Strongest indicators of phishing emails |
| 3 | Spear phishing vs. generic phishing |
| 4 | USB baiting & physical social engineering |
| 5 | Fake website detection (homograph attacks) |
| 6 | Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) benefits |
| 7 | Business Email Compromise (BEC) / Whaling |
| 8 | Recognizing legitimate vs. phishing email traits |
| 9 | Smishing (SMS phishing) awareness |
| 10 | Incident response — what to do after an attack |
